POPIA and privacy

Connect-ZA processes personal information only to create accounts, verify businesses, display approved listings, manage quotes, messages, PRIME subscriptions, support, fraud checks, and legal compliance.

  • Users may request access, correction, deletion, or export of their personal information.
  • Verification documents are used for business approval and are not shown publicly.
  • Security incidents involving personal information will be handled under POPIA breach-notification duties.
  • Privacy requests: privacy@connect-za.com

Legal requirements

Platform use must comply with applicable South African laws including POPIA Act 4 of 2013, PAIA Act 2 of 2000 where applicable, the Consumer Protection Act 68 of 2008, and the Electronic Communications and Transactions Act 25 of 2002.

  • Businesses must provide accurate company, service, pricing, contact, address, and verification details.
  • No fraudulent listings, spam, misleading pricing, fake reviews, illegal services, or misuse of customer data.
  • Customers and businesses remain responsible for their own quotes, contracts, invoices, taxes, licences, permits, and service delivery.
  • Reported accounts may be reviewed, limited, suspended, or removed by admin.

Terms and conditions

By using Connect-ZA, users agree that the platform helps customers find and contact businesses, but does not guarantee workmanship, availability, pricing, payment outside the platform, or outcomes of direct customer-business agreements.

  • Standard subscriptions cost R150 per month and keep approved businesses listed normally.
  • PRIME subscriptions cost R250 per month and add boosted visibility, a PRIME badge, and highlighted placement.
  • Subscription payment does not override admin approval, verification, moderation, or suspension rights.
  • Reviews must be honest, based on real customer experiences, and may be moderated when abusive or false.
  • Continued use of the platform means acceptance of these terms and future policy updates.
